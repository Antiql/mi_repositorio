-Our story-

What inspired us to make this organisation was the recent increase in popularity "nightmare fuel" media gained.
Lately, horror movies, disturbing stories, crime cases are easily distributed across the internet (and quickly consumed).
On top of that, you can appreciate this idea of creating your own creepy project (movie, drawing, book) among horror lovers very often.
Anyone can be an artist as long as they put their mind and concentration profusely into that project, so why not help make their dreamt nightmares true?

Our purpose

We want to serve as a space of horror sharing, where anyone can show their deepest fears to others, tell their craziest and horrific stories, within the presence of a group of artists that "horrify" your inputs
